Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Yardvark ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information when you use the Yardvark application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and name through our authentication provider, Auth0.

Lawn Care Data: We store the content you create within the app, including lawn care entries, uploaded photos, equipment records, product information, and soil test results.

Location Data: We collect location information you provide in Settings to retrieve local weather forecasts and soil temperature data. This is used solely to provide those features.

Usage Data: We use LogRocket to collect session recordings and error reports to help us diagnose bugs and improve the app. This may include information about how you interact with the app and your device and browser type.

Payment Information: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your payment card details — Stripe handles all payment data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service
  • To display your lawn care data, analytics, and AI-generated insights
  • To retrieve location-based weather and soil temperature information
  • To process subscription payments
  • To monitor app performance, diagnose issues, and improve features
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with the following third-party service providers as necessary to operate the Service:

  • Auth0: Handles account authentication and identity management
  • Stripe: Processes subscription payments
  • LogRocket: Session recording and error monitoring for app improvement

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at yardvarkapp@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure, industry-standard authentication via Auth0. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Yardvark is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at yardvarkapp@gmail.com.
